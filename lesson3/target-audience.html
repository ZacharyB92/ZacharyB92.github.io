My Target Audience

My target adience is families. I am looking to reach out to families looking to go outsite and play some games out in the yard or go to the park and hang out.

John and Jane Doe

  • John wants to take his kids to the park to play on the playground but is unsure whether the weather is good enough to go. He pulls up the lawn chair weather site and see that it is good to go!
  • Jane wants to go in the back yard and sit in her lawn chair to get some sun. She has been inside watching a movie all day and has no clue what its like outside. She pulls up the weather site and breaks out her shades.
  • John and Jane want to call over some freinds in a couple days to play volleyball in their yard. John and Jane don't know what the weather will be like. They pull up the site and pick a day that would be good.
  • John wants to invite the guys over for a BBQ. He checks the weather site and decides to have the bros over.
  • Jane wants to invite her kid's friends over to play in the yard. She checks the site to make sure there are no storms coming.
